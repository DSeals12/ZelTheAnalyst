About Me

Want to know a little more?
Below I will tell you a little bit more about my background.

I was born and raised in Houston, TX (Pearland to be exact). Played Football, Basketball and track all throughout High School. I have one older brother. I got my Bachelors of Science in Statistics and Data Science from The University of Texas at San Antonio (Go Runners!). Fun fact, in college I was apart of a group that won the national championship in intramural flag football.

How I got into data? Well I always had a thing for numbers but I got more into technology in college more so when I saw my brother coding. I thought it was really fascinating and wanted to see what could be done with data. Upon taking some college courses my senior year I was overwhelmed by how much you could create from coding thus bringing out my passion for Data Analytics. Since College I've had jobs as an Analyst for a Cost Recovery company, my next gig was handling the pricing and data analytics for an Aerospace company, and now I handle the financial strategy/data analytics for a Fintech company named Bread Financial. Some of the various tools I have used are SQL, Excel, Tableau, Python and SAP.
